// why this exists

Going dark is the new normal.

Iran has been going dark for over two decades: throttling, blackouts, whole protocols banned overnight. It is not alone. State shutdowns are now a routine lever of control, and this has been the worst year on record. MoaV began as one person's answer to a filter that kept winning.

2019Nationwide blackout, roughly a week fully dark

Automatic failover

Every endpoint is probed end to end through its own tunnel. When one drops, the balancer moves you to the next healthy one, no reconnect.

Modular by design

Every protocol is a sidecar container. Pick only the ones you want at install, and add your own later without touching the core.
